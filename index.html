Services

PageKey Solutions develops custom websites and web applications suited to the needs of each individual customer. Depending on your business needs, this can include anything from a single site to a complex online system that will streamline your business processes.

Static Website

A static website is an informational website that will provide your customers with a glimpse of your business or project. This website can include any information you desire, including images, text, videos, and links to other content.

Web Application

A web application is a sophisticated online tool. It is flexible and can include features to automate your business, including user accounts, emailing, input / organization of data, and much more. To find out more about web applications and discover how it can help you meet your goals, contact us below.

Search Engine Optimization

What's the use of a website if no one visits it? Search Engine Optimization is a service that will keep customers viewing your site and bringing you busienss. The purpose of Search Engine Optimization is to make your ranking on various search engines rise, making it more likely that potentially customers will find you when they are looking for the product or service that you provide.
